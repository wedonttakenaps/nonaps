Mirrored Systems Act II

NONAPS was selected for the Village Artist in Residency program with the Asian Art Museum during the summer of 2018. This was the second of three installations delivered over the course of the residency. Reflective inflatable tubes were reintroduced to the site but were then surrounded by a perimeter of matte black columns. The tubes could still be moved and manipulated but were restricted to the confines of the boundary. This barrier separated those outside from those within creating a more private and detached experience.

The materials used were 2 mil MET PET/LLDPE tubing, 2 mil mylar film, sonotubes, packing tape, and artificial grass.
